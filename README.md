## What is Nawah Control?
Nawah Control is a **cash-first commerce control layer** designed to reduce losses in COD-based e-commerce by managing:
- Cash on Delivery (COD)
- Shipping decisions
- Trust and risk
- Returns and non-payment
- Address and last-mile issues

It is built for **Yemen, Gulf countries, and similar markets** where:
- Electronic payment gateways are unreliable or inaccessible
- Addresses are often unclear
- COD remains the dominant payment method
