## What is Nawah Control?

**Nawah Control** is a **cash-first commerce control layer** built to reduce operational losses in **Cash-on-Delivery (COD)** e-commerce.

Rather than replacing payment gateways or acting as a bank, Nawah Control introduces a **decision and risk control layer** that operates *after the order is placed and before losses occur*.

---

## The Problem We Address

In many emerging and regional markets â€” including **Yemen, Gulf countries, and parts of the Arab world** â€” e-commerce faces systemic issues:

- Electronic payment gateways are unreliable or inaccessible
- A large segment of users cannot complete online payments
- Addresses are often incomplete or non-standardized
- COD remains the dominant payment method
- High rates of:
  - Delivery refusal
  - Non-payment at delivery
  - Returns and reverse logistics losses

> The result: merchants absorb operational losses with no control layer.

---

## The Nawah Control Solution

Nawah Control introduces an **operational control infrastructure** that enables merchants to:

- Evaluate risk **before shipping**
- Make informed shipping decisions
- Track delivery outcomes as financial signals
- Reduce COD-related losses without changing customer behavior
- Operate independently of banks or global payment platforms

It works **with reality**, not against it.

---

## Key Capabilities

- COD operational management
